One-to-one Mindfulness Support

Personal, confidential support for navigating stress, pressure or transition

I work with individuals on a one-to-one basis, offering space to slow things down and develop a steadier, more skilful relationship with stress, pressure, and thinking patterns.

This includes delivery of an 8-week mindfulness course, tailored to the individual and their working life. Many organisations choose to fund this work as part of their employee wellbeing provision.

What the 8-week course includes

The course is structured but flexible, designed to meet you where you are and work with the realities of your life. Each session builds on the last, introducing practical tools and approaches that you can use immediately.

We work together to:

  • • Understand your habitual patterns of thinking and reacting
  • • Develop practical mindfulness tools for managing stress and pressure
  • • Create space to respond rather than react
  • • Build sustainable practices that fit your daily life
  • • Work with difficulty in a more skilful way

Sessions are confidential, calm, and focused on what matters to you. There is no pressure to be a certain way or achieve specific outcomes. This is about learning to work with your mind more effectively, in your own time.

Who is one-to-one support for?

This approach may be particularly helpful if you:

  • • Are experiencing ongoing stress, pressure or overwhelm
  • • Are going through a period of change or transition
  • • Want to understand your patterns and reactions more clearly
  • • Prefer individual support over group settings
  • • Want a structured approach but with flexibility to focus on what matters to you

The work is practical, grounded, and designed to fit around your working life — not add to it.

Funding options

One-to-one mindfulness support can be:

  • • Self-funded — you pay directly for the course
  • • Employer-funded — many organisations choose to fund this work as part of their employee wellbeing provision

If you're interested in employer-funded support, I'm happy to provide information that can be shared with your HR or wellbeing team.

Interested in one-to-one support?

If you'd like to discuss whether this approach might be right for you, or if you have questions about the course, you're very welcome to get in touch.

There is no pressure, just a conversation.
