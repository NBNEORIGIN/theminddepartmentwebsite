Group Classes

Gentle, accessible mindfulness practice in a supportive group setting

These classes offer a gentle, accessible way to practise mindfulness in a supportive group setting.

They're designed for people who want to bring mindfulness into their week without the commitment or cost of one-to-one work, as well as those who value practising alongside others.

You may find these classes particularly supportive if you:

  • • Want to add mindfulness to your existing movement or training routine
  • • Are returning to movement after injury or illness
  • • Are currently managing illness, injury, or fatigue and need something gentle
  • • Have completed a one-to-one mindfulness course and would like ongoing support
  • • Simply want space to slow down, reconnect, and breathe

Classes combine gentle mindful movement with guided meditation, focusing on awareness, ease, and listening to your own body. Everything is offered as an invitation — there is no pressure to push, perform, or keep up.

You're always encouraged to work within your own limits, rest when needed, and adapt practices so they feel right for you on the day.

These sessions are not about fixing or improving yourself. They are about creating space to be present, steady, and kind to what's already here.

Group classes can stand alone, or sit alongside one-to-one work as a way to continue and deepen your practice over time.

8-Week Group Mindfulness Courses

From time to time, I also offer 8-week group mindfulness courses.

These courses follow a structured programme, allowing space to explore mindfulness more deeply and steadily over time, while still benefiting from the shared experience of a small group.

Group courses may be a good fit if you:

  • • Would like a more consistent, guided mindfulness practice
  • • Value learning alongside others, without pressure to share more than feels comfortable
  • • Are looking for a supportive next step beyond classes
  • • Prefer a structured course but don't need one-to-one support

Because these courses require commitment and continuity, they are not run throughout the year. Groups are kept intentionally small to maintain a calm, supportive environment.

If you're interested in joining a future course, you're very welcome to get in touch. I'm happy to let you know when the next group is planned, or to talk through whether this format would suit you.
